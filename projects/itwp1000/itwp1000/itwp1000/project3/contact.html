About the Author

My name is Mallory, and I have lived in the same house in St. Clair Shores my entire life. I attended Lake Shore high school in St. Clair Shores, Michigan. I completed three AP classes between my junior and senior years, and was dual enrolled at Macomb Community college senior year. I was a member of the National Honor Society, National Society of High School Scholars, Key Club, Debate Club, and Science Olympiad.
