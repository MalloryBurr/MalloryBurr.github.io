Topics:


Forests

Michigan boasts some of the country's most diverse and beautiful forests. Animals are allowed in the pictures, but must be present in a forest setting (not a zoo).


Grasslands

There are acres and acres of farmland to be found in the south eastern regions of Michigan and the surrounding states. These rolling grasslands are simple, but still breath taking.


Fresh Water - lakes and rivers

Michigan is best known for the incredible abundance of fresh water within the state. From creeks, to rivers, to ponds, to the amazing Great Lakes, there are countless opportunities for fresh water photos.
