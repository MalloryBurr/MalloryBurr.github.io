Welcome to the Macomb Community College Photo Contest

This website is for the Macomb Community College Photo Contest. Aspiring and amateur photographers welcome. This contest was formed in 2014 by Steve Yzerman and Henrik Zetterberg. We believe, as they do, that photography is a beautiful and inspiring hobby. We pass by beauty every day, and it takes a sharp eye to notice the correct angle from which life is best captured in a photo.

This website is a hub to submit, view, and recognize current and past applicants. Although many of the photographers that take part in our contest are not professional photographers yet, this is a great way to interact with other photographers and get a good look at the way the profession runs.
